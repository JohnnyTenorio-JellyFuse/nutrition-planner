6 Rules for Muscle Preservation
🐟
Shrimp or Fish at Every Lunch
Shrimp over squash noodles = 53g protein in one meal. Make it your default.
🥚
Egg Whites Are Your Protein Hack
2 egg whites = 7g protein, 34 cal, near-zero fat. Add to any scramble, any meal.
🌊
Salmon Twice a Week Minimum
EPA + DHA omega-3s reduce MBF inflammation and speed recovery between sessions.
🚫
No Grains or Bread at Dinner
Fish and vegetables only. Carbs at night get stored, not burned.
💧
96 oz Water Daily
192 ÷ 2 = 96 oz. Start with 16 oz the moment you wake at 4am.
⏰
Protein Every 3–4 Hours
5 meals spaced 3–4 hours apart keeps muscle protein synthesis elevated all day.
